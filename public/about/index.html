EDUCATION

The University of Chicago

MS Analytics, 2016

BA Economics, East Asian Language and Civilizations, 2010

TECHNICAL SUMMARY

R, MS Office (high proficiency in Excel, Access), Tableau, Git/GitHub, SQL, QGIS, Base SAS

Data Analyst
